About Us

Founded in 2003 in Sangam Vihar, Delhi,Allure Pharma has established itself as a premier Manufacturer and Wholesaler in the pharmaceutical industry. We specialize in a comprehensive range of high-quality products, including pharmaceutical tablets, capsules, syrups, and more.

Our commitment to quality is unwavering; we source our products exclusively from trusted and renowned vendors, ensuring that each item meets stringent industry standards. We pride ourselves on offering competitive pricing and timely delivery, making us a reliable partner for all your pharmaceutical needs.

In addition to our manufacturing capabilities, we also provide exceptional Third Party Manufacturing and PCD Pharma Franchise opportunities, tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients. Under the leadership of our CEO, Mr. Anil Kumar Gupta, we have cultivated a diverse and loyal clientele across the nation. At Allure Pharma, we are dedicated to fostering long-lasting relationships built on trust, quality, and excellence.
